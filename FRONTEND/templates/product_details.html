Power Output:

Wattage: Higher wattage means faster charging. Standard USB ports typically offer 5W, while fast chargers can offer 18W, 30W, or even higher.

Amperage: Measured in amps (A), standard USB ports offer 1A or 2.1A. Fast chargers might offer 3A or more.

Compatibility:

Ensure the charger is compatible with your device’s charging standard (e.g., Quick Charge, Power Delivery).

Check the voltage range to ensure it fits your car’s power system (usually 12V or 24V).

Build Quality and Safety:

Look for chargers with over-current, over-voltage, over-temperature, and short-circuit protection to ensure safe charging.

High-quality materials and a durable design are important for long-term use.
